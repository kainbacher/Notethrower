Oneloudr

is a platform for musicians to collaborate in the music making, discovery and selling process. Artists create music with each other in a co-ownership arrangement. We simplify the process of making great music together and creating ways to get paid for your original music.

We are a Community for Co-Writers.

Oneloudr gives everyone a chance to share in the creative process of making music. If you have access to your favorite digital recording software like (Pro Tools, Logic, Cubase, Garageband, etc.), and an internet connection, you can record your musical ideas and upload your bounced audio tracks and mixes to oneloudr. Other artists will have the opportunity to download and expand on your original work and then upload their contributions to your artist profile.

Private or Public Tracks

Artist's have the choice of sharing their "work-in-progress" with the entire oneloudr community, or limiting the song creation process to their select list of collaborators. If your last band broke up because your drummer moved across the country, simply connect on oneloudr, and share your recording sessions. Now there is no excuse for not finishing that hit that never made it off your hard drive.

Get Started!

As an artist or band, you can update your profile when you need to. We will never spam you, or give out your email address to third parties.

We are currently operating in "beta" mode. This means you are an early adopter of our service and know a good thing when you see one, but it also means that you may occasionally experience small "bugs" on the site. When this happens, we would appreciate your feedback by simply clicking on the feedback button on the left side of the screen on the tracks page. This way we can address the problem quickly. If you have any other suggestions on how to get the most use out of our service, please let us know when a great idea hits you.

We're happy you found us, and we look forward to providing the best way for musicians to create and earn together online!
